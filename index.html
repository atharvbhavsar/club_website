WHY JOIN US?

TOP FAQS

1. What should I wear for the event? Wear comfortable clothing suitable for dance activities.

2. Do I need prior dance experience to participate?Not at all! Our events are open to all skill levels, from beginners to experienced dancers. Specific sessions for advanced techniques may be mentioned in the event details.

3. Is there a registration fee for the event? Some events may be free, while others may have a nominal registration fee. Details about fees and payment methods will be included in the event description.

4. How can I register for the event? You can register by filling out the form linked on our Events page. If registration is required, a "Sign Up" on "VIERP" App will be provided

5. Can non-club members join the events? Yes! Many of our events are open to the entire college community. Some exclusive workshops or performances may be limited to club members, so check the event details.

6. What happens if I miss the registration deadline? Unfortunately, we may not be able to accommodate late registrations due to limited spots. Make sure to sign up before the deadline to secure your place.

ABOUT US

Welcome to Zephyr, the official dance club of Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune! We dance to the beats of our hearts, embodying passion, energy, and creativity in every performance

◉ Our Journey

Since our inception, Zephyr has been a vibrant community for dancers of all styles and skill levels. We believe in the power of dance to unite, inspire, and transform, fostering an environment where creativity thrives.

◉ Mission and Vision

Since our inception, Zephyr has been a vibrant community for dancers of all styles and skill levels. We believe in the power of dance to unite, inspire, and transform, fostering an environment where creativity thrives.

◉ What We Do

What We Do Workshops and Training: We conduct regular workshops covering various dance styles, including hip-hop, contemporary, classical, and more. These sessions are designed for dancers at all levels, from beginners to advanced performers. Performances and Competitions: Zephyr actively participates in cultural festivals and competitions. Notably, we were the 1st Runner Up at IIT Bombay's Mood Indigo 'Desi Beats' in 2023. INSTAGRAM Community Engagement: We host events and workshops to engage with the broader community, sharing our love for dance and encouraging others to join us in this artistic journey.
